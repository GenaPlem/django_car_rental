Rental Rules

Driving License: A valid driving license with a minimum of 2 years of driving experience is required.

Age-Based Insurance:

  • Ages 20-25: Young Driver Insurance
  • Ages 25-50: Standard Driver Insurance
  • Ages 50+: Senior Driver Insurance

Mileage Limit: Each rental comes with a standard daily mileage limit. Excess mileage will incur additional charges.

Fuel Policy: Vehicles must be returned with the same amount of fuel as at the start of the rental to avoid refueling charges.

Additional Drivers: Must be registered at the time of rental and meet the same requirements as the primary driver.

Damage Policy: Any damage to the vehicle not covered by insurance will be the responsibility of the renter.

Late Return: Late returns may be subject to extra day charges at the daily rate.

Booking Cancellation: Free cancellation up to 48 hours before the pick-up time. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

No Smoking: All vehicles are non-smoking. A cleaning fee will be charged if this rule is violated.

Prohibited Use: Vehicles cannot be used for off-roading, racing, or any illegal activities.

Geographical Restrictions: Vehicles must not be taken out of the predefined rental area without prior approval.

Payment: Rental charges must be paid in full at the time of vehicle pick-up.

Vehicle Condition: The renter agrees to maintain the vehicle's condition, reporting any mechanical issues immediately.

Emergency Procedures: In case of an accident or emergency, renters must follow the outlined procedures provided at the time of rental.
